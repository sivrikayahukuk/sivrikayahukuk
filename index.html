Compliance Tools

Sanction Double-Checker & Fact-Checking Expert

Cross-check entities against multi-jurisdictional sanctions lists and verify claims against curated facts. Outputs are linked through a shared evidence panel.

Illustrative sample data. Not a substitute for production sanctions screening or legal advice.

Structured · Sanction Lists

Sanction Double-Checker

Screens an entity name against OFAC SDN, EU Consolidated, UN 1267, and UK OFSI samples. Hits on a single list are flagged "needs corroboration"; hits on two or more are marked "corroborated".

Unstructured · Claims

Fact-Checking Expert

Verifies a free-text claim against a curated knowledge base. Returns a verdict with sources and the entities it extracted from your claim.

Cross-linked evidence

Evidence Bridge

Entities from the fact-check are auto-screened. Sanction hits surface any related claims. Each row links structured (sanction) ↔ unstructured (claim) information.

Run a screen or a fact-check to populate the bridge.
